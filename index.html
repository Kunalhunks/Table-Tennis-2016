ABOUT  THE  TOURNAMENT

The 18th annual Ajay Kumar Garg Memorial Table Tennis Tournament is to be conducted on 14th and 15th November 2014 at Ajay Kumar Garg Engineering College in association with the Ghaziabad District Table Tennis Association. The tournament is held every year in an air-conditioned hall, contains simultaneous matches on various tables. Teams from NCR, Haryana, Rajasthan, Uttarakhand, UP, and Punjab mark their participation in 10 different categories ranging from 'Under 18' to 'Over 40' for men and women separately in individual and team events. A huge amount is kept as the prize money.

Eligibility

  • All Professional Institutes like Medical, Engineering, Management, B.Ed, Computer Applications etc.
  • Any graduate/Post Graduate College.
  • Organisations (Govt./ Semi Govt./ banks)
  • Entry for individual events open to Men and Women team members and their extra players
  • All Boys and Girls Schools.
  • Entry for individual events open to Men and Women team members and their extra players.
